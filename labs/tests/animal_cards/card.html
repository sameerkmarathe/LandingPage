Dog

The dog is man's best friend. He has the following attributes:

  • Scientific Name: Canis Canidae
  • Average Weight: 50lbs
  • Varieties: Over 4,000
  • Uses: As working dogs (assistants), or general pets, dogs are devoted to their human masters in all situations, and can be trained to do most anything.

A dog is a wonderful companion. If we take the proper steps to keep our senior dogs healthy, they can remain active and happy well into the equivalent of their 80s or 90s.
